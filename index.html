Little About Us

Together we can
make a difference

MAITHREE is a movement by the parents, of the parents and for the parents endeavouring to provide a secure future to Children/Persons with special needs.

Maithree has transformed the lives of more than 3000 families through its single-minded services for education, vocational training for skill development. Different vocations are propagated through sheltered engagement and residential care in conjunction with parental counseling for awareness and acceptance.

Our Programmes

Education

Educational Programmes

Maithree has developed a structured special education programme that is designed to augment the inherent potential of a child with Intellectual Disability ranging right from its birth to eighteen years of age.

Vocation

Vocational training Skilling

We strongly believes that all Persons with Intellectual and Developmental Disabilities irrespective of their degree of disability can become productive, provided they get an appropriate and suitable opportunity, thereby empowering them economically.

Vocation

Vocational - Sheltered Engagement

We make decisions and choices in our day to life as well as in our interaction with the society at large including work place, public utilities etc., without recognizing that the effectiveness of all our decisions or choices are dependent on the Discretionary Ability of ours. Don’t we look for support or assistance when our discretionary abilities are challenged?

Residential

Residential Care

Our residential facilities is unique as they cater to the individual requirement and personalities of the residents; each of these homes are safe, peaceful, and comfortable and is a Home away from Home.

Aakruthi

Aakruthi Social Enterprise

Aakruti is the Social enterprise brand of Maithree – an association of parents of exceptional children. It functions as an institution committed to the welfare of persons with special needs.
