Residential Care

Each of our residential facilities is unique as they cater to the individual requirement and personalities of the residents; each of these homes are safe, peaceful, and comfortable and is a Home away from Home.


Maithree provides high-quality support services like personal hygiene, dressing, meal preparation, household chores, vocational training, recreation, and social skill building to help each resident lead an independent life at the home and in the community as well as administering the medical needs.


Apart from residential care, Maithree provides various opportunities such as Farming, Gardening, Weaving and Arts & Crafts, Support staff are available on hand 24/7 to assist the residents with everything from medication to grocery shopping in the community.


The residential home for Men is located in a rural farm neighbourhood at Vembakkam, Tiruvannamalai District, a village near Kanchipuram, 90kms from Chennai. The home for women is in the heart of Chennai city at Perambur.
